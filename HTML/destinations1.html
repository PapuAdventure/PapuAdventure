Raja Ampat

Welcome to Raja Ampat, a breathtaking archipelago nestled in the heart of the Coral Triangle. Discover unparalleled natural beauty, vibrant marine life, and rich cultural heritage in this remote paradise.

Explore the unparalleled beauty and rich cultural heritage of Raja Ampat, nestled off the northwest tip of Bird’s Head Peninsula in West Papua, Indonesia, renowned for its staggering biodiversity with over 1,500 fish species and 600 coral species spread across its 1,500 islands, cays, and shoals; discover iconic attractions like the Wayag Islands with their breathtaking karst formations, Misool Island’s pristine beaches and world-class diving sites, and Piaynemo’s Instagram-worthy viewpoints; indulge in thrilling activities such as diving amidst vibrant coral gardens, snorkeling in crystal-clear waters, kayaking through hidden bays, trekking in lush jungles, and immersing yourself in the unique customs and traditions of the indigenous Papuan people in their traditional villages.
